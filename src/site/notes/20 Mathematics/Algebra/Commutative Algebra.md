# Commutative Algebra

Commutative algebra studies [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Atiyah-Macdonald/01 Rings and Ideals/01 Rings and Ring Homomorphisms/Ring\|commutative rings]], their [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Atiyah-Macdonald/01 Rings and Ideals/02 Ideals and Quotient Rings/Ideal\|ideals]], and their [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Atiyah-Macdonald/02 Modules/02.01 Modules and Module Homomorphisms/Module\|modules]]. It provides the algebraic language behind algebraic geometry and much of modern number theory.
