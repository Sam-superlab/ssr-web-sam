# Fields and Extensions

Fields are the simplest nonzero commutative rings from the viewpoint of ideals. Extensions enlarge a base ring or field while preserving enough arithmetic structure to compare elements, ideals, and prime spectra.

## Fields and domains

- [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Rings and Morphisms/Elements and Special Rings/Field\|Field]] is characterized ideal-theoretically by [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Rings and Morphisms/Elements and Special Rings/Characterizations of a Field\|Characterizations of a Field]].
- [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Rings and Morphisms/Elements and Special Rings/Integral Domain\|Integral Domain]] is the natural starting point for fraction fields.
- [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Rings and Morphisms/Elements and Special Rings/Principal Ideal Domain\|Principal Ideal Domain]] supplies a particularly tractable class of domains.
- [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Localization and Fractions/Fraction Rings/Field of Fractions\|Field of Fractions]] is the smallest field obtained by inverting every nonzero element of a domain.
- [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Localization and Fractions/Fraction Rings/Total Ring of Fractions\|Total Ring of Fractions]] extends the fraction construction to rings with zero divisors.

## Algebraic extensions

- [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Fields and Extensions/Core/Algebraic Closure\|Algebraic Closure]] records the canonical definition, existence, and uniqueness statement.
- [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Integral Extensions and Normalization/Integral Elements and Extensions/Integral Element\|Integral Element]] is the ring-theoretic analogue of an algebraic element.
- [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Integral Extensions and Normalization/Integral Elements and Extensions/Integral Extension\|Integral Extension]] collects extensions in which every element is integral.
- [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Integral Extensions and Normalization/Integral Elements and Extensions/Integral Dependence\|Integral Dependence]] gives useful finite-generation and equation criteria.
- [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Fields and Extensions/Core/Artin Construction of an Algebraic Closure\|Artin Construction of an Algebraic Closure]] gives an existence construction for algebraic closures of fields.

## Closure and normality

- [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Integral Extensions and Normalization/Integral Closure and Normality/Integral Closure\|Integral Closure]] gathers all elements integral over a base ring inside a larger ring.
- [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Integral Extensions and Normalization/Integral Closure and Normality/Integrally Closed Domain\|Integrally Closed Domain]] is a domain equal to its integral closure in its fraction field.
- [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Valuation Theory/Valuation Rings and Integral Closure/Valuation Ring\|Valuation Ring]] provides a local model of divisibility.
- [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Valuation Theory/Valuation Rings and Integral Closure/Valuative Criterion for Integral Closure\|Valuative Criterion for Integral Closure]] characterizes integral closure using valuation rings.

## Prime ideals across extensions

- [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Integral Extensions and Normalization/Prime Spectra under Integral Extensions/Lying-Over Theorem\|Lying-Over Theorem]] guarantees primes above primes in an integral extension.
- [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Integral Extensions and Normalization/Prime Spectra under Integral Extensions/Incomparability Theorem\|Incomparability Theorem]] controls primes with the same contraction.
- [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Integral Extensions and Normalization/Prime Spectra under Integral Extensions/Going-Up Theorem\|Going-Up Theorem]] lifts chains of prime ideals.
- [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Integral Extensions and Normalization/Prime Spectra under Integral Extensions/Going-Down Theorem\|Going-Down Theorem]] lifts chains in the opposite direction under additional hypotheses.
- [[20 Mathematics/Algebra/Commutative Algebra/Concepts/Integral Extensions and Normalization/Integral Closure and Normality/Radical of an Extended Ideal under Integral Closure\|Radical of an Extended Ideal under Integral Closure]] connects extension of ideals with radicals.
