## What is median
>The **median** is another name for the 50th [[20 Mathematics/Probability and Statistics/percentile\|percentile]]. It is frequently used as a measure of center

These are some other ways to think about the median. 
- the median divides the data into an upper and lower half 
- the middle value of the data if arranged from smallest to largest 
- about half the data is larger than the median and about half the data is smaller

### properties
1. Median is [[20 Mathematics/Probability and Statistics/robust\|robust]] to data
2. Median often used in [[20 Mathematics/Probability and Statistics/Order Statistics\|Order Statistics]]
