## Definition of Econ

The study of how individuals and societies allocate their limited resources to satisfy their practically unlimited wants by Matter and Coppock

OR

The social science that studies the production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services. 

OR

The study of choice under conditions of scarcity 

OR

The study of how people interact with each other and with their natural surroundings in producing their livlihoods...


## Scarcity

>The inherently limited nature of society's resources given society's unlimited wants and needs.

**Some examples:** 
- Time
- money
- etc.

***Scarcity forces people to make trade-offs***


## Two major branches of Economics
- Micro
	- The study of the individual units that make up an economy
	- individuals, businesses, markets
- Macro
	- The study of the overall aspects and workings of an economy
	- country-level fluctuations in the economy
