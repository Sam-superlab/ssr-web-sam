### Set Operations(Also see [[Mainfolder/Mathematics/Mathematics Topics/Set theory\|Set theory]])
- Union(Or)
	- $P(A\ or\ B)=P(A \cup B)=P(A)+P(B)-P(A\cap B)$
- Intersection(And)
	- $P(A\ and\ B)=P(A \cap B)$
- Complementary(Not)
	- $P(A^c)=1-P(A)$
- Disjoint
	- Two events are disjoint if $P(A \cap B)=\emptyset$
